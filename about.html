Zayed.

While other kids played video games, Zayed was building his first computer systems — back when dial-up modems screeched and floppy disks were revolutionary. That childhood fascination became a 15+ year track record of driving measurable business growth through digital innovation.

At Ogilvy & Mather

Zayed didn't just manage projects — he delivered results for industry giants like Google, Cisco, and Unilever. Coordinating with teams across three continents, he learned that successful digital transformation requires more than technical skills. It demands clear communication, adaptable problem-solving, and the ability to navigate complex stakeholder landscapes. These aren't just soft skills — they're the difference between projects that launch and projects that convert.

At InterContinental Hotels Group

Managing 65 million member communications isn't for the faint of heart. As Guest Campaign Manager across Asia, Middle East, and Australia, Zayed orchestrated multilingual campaigns in Arabic, Korean, and Japanese that contributed to a 5% increase in member activation. His clients don't just get websites; they get complete operational overhauls that turn traditional retail models into revenue-generating digital engines.

As a Shopify ecosystem expert, Zayed brings enterprise-level implementation strategies typically reserved for Fortune 500 companies to businesses ready to scale. His recommendations don't just optimise your store — they impact the bottom line from day one.
