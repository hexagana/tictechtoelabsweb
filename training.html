▸ FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

How many people can attend a session?

Workshop format works best with 8–30 pax. For lecture-style "talks", we've delivered to audiences of 130+ comfortably. We'll recommend the right format based on your goals.

Do you deliver outside Singapore?

Yes. On-site globally for the right brief, and virtual delivery as standard. Recent sessions have run across APAC, Middle East, and EU.

Can you customise content to our tech stack?

That's actually the default — most of our engagements start with a needs analysis and get tailored from there. We adapt to MS365, Google Workspace, Shopify, HubSpot, and most common business tooling.

How do you price?

Day-rate based, scaled to cohort size and content depth. Indicative pricing comes in your proposal after a short discovery call. No surprises, no hidden consulting fees.

Do you issue certificates?

Yes — participants receive a Tic Tech Toe Labs certificate of completion. We can also align sessions with SkillsFuture claims where applicable (ask us).

What's the free needs analysis, really?

A 10-question form, a short follow-up call if useful, then a short PDF report from us: where your team is, where the quick wins are, and which programmes (ours or not) would actually move the needle. No sales pitch in the report itself.
