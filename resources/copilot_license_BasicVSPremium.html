From free Windows features to full enterprise integration

Free Version

All Windows Users

  • No advanced M365 app integrations

Personal, Family & Premium
  • Draft and summarize documents

  • File Explorer integration for document Q&A

M365 Business

Enterprise Security + Web Chat
  • Enterprise Data Protection — queries not used for AI training

  • Image generation capabilities

  • Deep organizational context across all apps

💡 Understanding the Difference

Think of hiring a research assistant to understand the key difference between Business and Copilot licenses:

📚 M365 Business License

Like hiring a smart assistant who has access to the public library (the Web) and a shredder to keep your requests private — but they don't know anything about your personal filing cabinet.

🔑 M365 Copilot License

Gives that assistant the key to your office filing cabinet (Microsoft Graph), allowing them to connect your public research with your private emails, calendar, and company history.

In Practice: Basic vs Premium, Department by Department

What each licence actually unlocks on the shop floor and in the office

Basic = Copilot Chat included with M365 Business (Tier 3) — a secure, tenant-safe assistant grounded in the web and files you paste in.   Premium = the M365 Copilot licence add-on (Tier 4) — grounded in your live company data across Outlook, Teams, Excel & SharePoint. Examples below are framed for a plastics-manufacturing business.
🏭

Production & Operations

Keep lines running and shifts coordinated
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Explain injection-moulding cycle vs. cooling time, or draft a shift-handover template.
  • Summarise a machine-guarding SOP or safety PDF you paste into the chat.
  • Turn a supervisor's rough notes into a clean daily production report.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Summarise last week's downtime reports from the Production Teams channel and rank the top 3 causes.
  • In Excel, analyse the live scrap-rate sheet and flag every mould trending over 4%.
  • Draft the shift handover straight from your actual Outlook emails and meeting recap.
🔬

Quality Control & Assurance

Catch defects, close the loop, prove compliance
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Draft a generic SPC explainer or a blank CAPA / 8D template.
  • Summarise a single SDS or spec sheet you paste in.
  • Rewrite an inspector's findings into clear, customer-ready language.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Draft a CAPA directly from the customer complaint email thread about warped ABS parts.
  • Compare a batch's dimensional data against the spec file in SharePoint and flag out-of-tolerance results.
  • Trend every non-conformance report this quarter and surface the repeat offenders.
🚚

Supply Chain & Procurement

Buy resin smarter, react to price moves faster
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Research typical resin lead times or draft a supplier RFQ template.
  • Turn three quotes you paste in into a clean comparison table.
  • Draft a chaser email to a late supplier.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Find every resin-supplier email this month mentioning price increases and summarise the cost impact.
  • Build a supplier comparison from the quotes already sitting in your inbox — no copy-paste.
  • Recap the procurement review meeting and list who owns each follow-up.
🔧

Maintenance & Engineering

Cut unplanned downtime, keep assets serviced
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Explain a preventive-maintenance schedule or interpret a pasted error-code list.
  • Summarise an uploaded machine manual into a one-page PM checklist.
  • Draft a work-order description from a technician's notes.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Query the maintenance-log SharePoint list: which extruders are overdue for service?
  • Cross-reference breakdown history from Teams to spot the most failure-prone assets.
  • Turn the OEM manual in the Engineering site into a tailored PM checklist automatically.
🦺

EHS, Safety & Compliance

Manage hazardous materials and stay audit-ready
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Draft a lockout/tagout toolbox talk or summarise one SDS at a time.
  • Explain OSHA or local regulatory requirements in plain language.
  • Rewrite an incident note into a formal report structure.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Pull key hazards from every SDS file in the Safety library for the chemicals on Line 2.
  • Summarise this quarter's incident reports from Teams and draft the safety-committee agenda.
  • Check a proposed procedure against the policies stored in SharePoint.
💰

Finance & Cost Control

Understand cost drivers and variances quickly
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Explain contribution margin or build a formula from a table you paste in.
  • Draft a budget-narrative or variance-commentary template.
  • Summarise a market report on resin pricing you paste in.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • In Excel, analyse the monthly production-cost workbook and explain the energy-cost-per-kg variance.
  • Recap the budget review meeting and assign each cost-reduction action to an owner.
  • Pull actuals from the finance workbook and draft the month-end commentary.
📞

Sales & Customer Service

Respond faster, never lose the thread
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Draft cold outreach, polish tone, or translate a customer email.
  • Write a product FAQ from material you paste in.
  • Summarise a long RFQ document you upload.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Catch up on the full email thread about the tooling delay and draft the reply for you.
  • Recap yesterday's customer call and log the action items automatically.
  • Pull order history from Outlook and draft a proactive status update.
👥

Human Resources

Handle policy, hiring and people questions
Basic · Copilot Chat
  • Draft a process-technician job description or summarise a policy you paste in.
  • Rewrite a manager's feedback into balanced review language.
  • Explain leave-entitlement concepts in plain terms.
Premium · M365 Copilot
  • Find the latest overtime policy in SharePoint and answer whether weekend shifts qualify.
  • Summarise candidate interview notes from the Teams hiring channel.
  • Draft a return-to-work letter grounded in the employee's case emails.
Rule of thumb: the more of someone's day is spent inside Outlook, Teams and Excel with real company data, the faster a Premium seat pays for itself. Give Basic to everyone; target Premium at planners, QA leads, procurement, engineering, finance, EHS and plant leadership.
